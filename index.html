about me

I am a UX Designer who offers unique solutions, that are creative, but also popular. My work stands out by having a personal note of my former background in arts and craft, service and literature studies, as they are inspiring, user-centered and well told.

When I am not busy with developing great UXD solutions, you will find me handcrafting whatever comes into my mind! I love to learn new techniques and to refine methods I already know. Next on my list is learning to code with HTML and CSS and to dive into the world of pottery.
